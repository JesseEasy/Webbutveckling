Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of the long-running anime/manga series One Piece created by Eiichiro Oda.

Brief Description:

Luffy is a young, optimistic, and incredibly determined pirate captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. As a boy, he accidentally ate the Devil Fruit called the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Gum-Gum Fruit), which turned his body into rubber, granting him elastic powers and immense stretching abilities (at the cost of never being able to swim). Known for his signature straw hat, red vest, and infectious laugh, Luffy is famous for his boundless energy, unbreakable sense of justice, and loyalty to his friends, whom he treats like family.


His Goal:

Luffy’s ultimate dream is to find the legendary treasure called the One Piece, left behind by the former Pirate King Gol D. Roger. By doing so, he aims to become the King of the Pirates and achieve complete freedom on the seas. He’s a simple, carefree character who lives by his own rules, values friendship above all, and inspires his crew to chase their dreams while facing powerful enemies, the World Government, and other pirate crews along the Grand Line.

Imu

Imu (Saint Nerona Imu) is one of the central antagonists and the ultimate hidden ruler in the One Piece series.

Brief Description:

Imu is the enigmatic and shadowy supreme leader of the World Government, often referred to as the "King of the World." They secretly occupy the Empty Throne in Mary Geoise (Marijoa), a position that symbolizes absolute authority, while the Five Elders act as the public face of power. As one of the original founders of the World Government from over 800 years ago, Imu's existence is a closely guarded secret from the rest of the world. They appear cold, ruthless, and god-like, viewing the entire planet as under their dominion and willing to erase any threats to their order without hesitation.


Their Goal:

Imu’s primary objective is to maintain the World Government’s ironclad control over the world, suppress forbidden history (particularly from the Void Century), and eliminate anyone connected to the "Will of D." or the ancient threat of Joy Boy. They stand as the ultimate embodiment of tyranny and stagnation, directly opposing ideals of freedom, adventure, and rebellion represented by Luffy and the Straw Hats. This makes Imu the overarching antagonist and final big bad of the series—mysterious, immensely powerful, and operating from the highest echelons of authority.
