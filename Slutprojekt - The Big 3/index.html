One Piece - 1150+ Episodes

One Piece is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy sets out on a grand adventure to find the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece" and become the Pirate King. Along the way, he assembles a diverse crew of pirates, each with unique abilities and dreams. The series is known for its rich world-building, complex characters, epic battles, and themes of friendship, freedom, and adventure. Since its debut in 1997, One Piece has become one of the best-selling manga series of all time and has a massive global fanbase.

Naruto - 720 Episodes

Naruto is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and leader of his village, the Hokage. Despite being an outcast due to the powerful Nine-Tails Fox spirit sealed inside him, Naruto trains hard and forms bonds with his friends and teammates. The series is renowned for its character development, intricate storylines, and intense battles. Since its debut in 1999, Naruto has become a global phenomenon and one of the best-selling manga series of all time.

Bleach - 366 Episodes

Bleach is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Tite Kubo. The story centers on Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper—a guardian of the afterlife—after meeting Rukia Kuchiki. With his new abilities, Ichigo must protect the living world from evil spirits known as Hollows and guide departed souls to the afterlife. The series is known for its action-packed battles, unique characters, and intricate plot. Since its debut in 2001, Bleach has garnered a large global fanbase and become one of the best-selling manga series.
